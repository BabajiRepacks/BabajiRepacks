Hello Neighbor


"Hello Neighbor" is a stealth horror game developed by Dynamic Pixels
and published by tinyBuild. In the game, you play as a character who
moves into a new house and becomes suspicious of the strange behavior
of your neighbor.

The goal is to sneak into the neighbor's house to uncover the secrets hidden
in their basement.The game is known for its AI system, which learns from
your actions and adapts to make it more challenging for you to sneak around.
It combines puzzle-solving with stealth and has a unique, cartoony
art style. The main objective is to avoid being caught by the
neighbor while solving puzzles to unlock different areas of the house.
